CineMap: A view of cinematography history.

The primary purpose of CineMap is to provide users with a way to visualize trends in film exhibition within the continental United States over the roughly 100-year history of feature-length motion pictures. CineMap will allow users to view information about individual theaters’ ownership and years of operation.

Languages/Tools: Node.js, Express 4, JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap

LyonTracks: A Google Glass App.

A Glass app designed and implemented in Python using the Mirror API. The app acts as an interactive tour that continually gives the user accurate and historical data of the various buildings at Wheaton College.

Interactive German Website: A new way to Learn Grammer.

An interactive website aimed at introductory level German speakers for the advancement and practice of German vocabulary and grammar. Implemented two-way and three-way matching card exercises, as well as fill in the blank and multiple choice activities.

Languages/Tools: HTML, CSS, jQuery and JavaScript
